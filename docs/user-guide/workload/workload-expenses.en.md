# **Workload Usage History**

You can check the detailed breakdown of points consumed based on your workload activity through the **"Usage Details"** feature. <br><br>

![Workload_Usage_History_20260106_01.jpg](img.en/Workload-Usage-History/Workload_Usage_History_20260106_01.jpg)

1\. Click the **'Usage Details'** button located at the bottom right of the workload item. <br><br>

![Workload_Usage_History_20260106_02.jpg](img.en/Workload-Usage-History/Workload_Usage_History_20260106_02.jpg)

2\. You can check the points consumed and the total usage fees for each workload item. <br><br>

![Workload_Usage_History_20260106_03.jpg](img.en/Workload-Usage-History/Workload_Usage_History_20260106_03.jpg)

3\. By clicking the **'Details'** button, you can check the granular billing breakdown by individual pod or usage duration.
