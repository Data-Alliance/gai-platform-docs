# **Home**

The Home menu allows you to monitor the overall cluster status of the current workloads.<br><br>

![Workload_Home_20260105_01.jpg](img.en/Home/Workload_Home_20260105_01.jpg)

1\. By clicking the **"Home"** menu on the left sidebar, you can view your total earned points, along with a summary and real-time status of your active workloads.<br><br>

![Workload_Home_20260105_02.jpg](img.en/Home/Workload_Home_20260105_02.jpg)

2\. You can view the overall operational summary of your created workloads, and by clicking on an individual workload description, you can access detailed information for each specific task.
