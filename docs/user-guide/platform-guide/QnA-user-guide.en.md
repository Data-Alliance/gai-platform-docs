# **QnA User Guide**

If you have any inquiries regarding the use of workloads or nodes, you can find answers through **Customer Support**<br><br>

## **Accessing the QnA Page**

Click on “Customer Support” located in the left sidebar menu.<br>
This service is available in both **Deploy Mode** and **Share Mode**. <br>

![QnA_User_Guide_20260106_01.jpg](img.en/QnA/QnA_User_Guide_20260106_01.jpg)

## **QnA FAQ Overview**

1\. FAQ categories are displayed on the QnA page<br>
2\. There are 6 categories: “All,” “GPU Rent,” “Payment,” “Technology,” “Account,” and “GPU Share.” <br>
The **top 3** frequently asked questions(FAQs) are displayed for each category. <br>
    
![QnA_User_Guide_20260106_02.jpg](img.en/QnA/QnA_User_Guide_20260106_02.jpg)
    
3\. If you cannot find the information you need in the FAQ, you can submit an inquiry by writing your own QnA. <br>
By selecting an Inquiry Type and, if necessary, specifying the Node or Workload in question, you can receive a more detailed response. <br>
Inquiries are received via email, and the answers will also be sent to your account's registered email address. <br>
At the bottom of the page, you will find links for the "Detailed FAQ" and the "KakaoTalk Channel." <br>
You can browse a more comprehensive FAQ list or use the KakaoTalk Channel for real-time inquiries.
