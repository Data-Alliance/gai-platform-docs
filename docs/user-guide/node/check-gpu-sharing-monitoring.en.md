# **Check GPU Sharing Monitoring**

You can monitor the real-time status of your GPU sharing activities through the Node interface.  <br><br>

![GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_01.jpg](img.en/gpu-sharing-monitoring/GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_01.jpg)

1\. Please click the **"Monitoring"** button on the GPU sharing information screen. <br><br>

![GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_02.jpg](img.en/gpu-sharing-monitoring/GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_02.jpg)

2\. As shown in the screen above, you can view the **complete node monitoring information**. <br><br>

![GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_03.jpg](img.en/gpu-sharing-monitoring/GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_03.jpg)

3\. By clicking on **"Select Time Range"**, you can review your node's past performance history broken down by specific time periods. <br><br>

![GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_04.jpg](img.en/gpu-sharing-monitoring/GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_04.jpg)

4\. By clicking on **"Custom Time Range"**, you can set a specific **"Start Time"** and **"End Time"** to view monitoring information only for your desired period. <br><br>

![GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_05.jpg](img.en/gpu-sharing-monitoring/GPU_Sharing_Monitoring_20260105_05.jpg)

5\. By clicking on **"Sampling Interval"**, you can adjust the time units of the graph. (Default is 10 minutes; you can set it from a minimum of 1 minute to a maximum of 5 hours.)
